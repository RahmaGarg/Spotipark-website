About Spotipark

Our smart parking app leverages Ethereum blockchain technology to provide a secure, transparent, and efficient parking management system. Users can easily find available parking spots, make payments, and track parking history through our intuitive Flutter-based mobile app.

Secure Transactions

Transactions are securely processed on the Ethereum blockchain using Ganache & MetaMask integration.

Real-time Parking Availability

Users can instantly find and reserve parking spots in real-time, reducing search time.

Efficient Payment System

Our app offers seamless payment processing, allowing users to pay for parking with ease.

Admin Dashboard

Administrators can manage parking spaces,users'accounts and monitor transactions through the admin dashboard.

Features

Explore the powerful features of our Smart Parking App powered by Ethereum blockchain technology. Simplify your parking experience with advanced functionalities.

Dynamic Pricing

Our app adapts parking rates dynamically, considering demand, time of day, and special events. Fixed prices are a thing of the past—our flexible system ensures fair charges for every user.

Proximity-Based Parking Categories

We’ve categorized parking spots based on their distance from the main entrance. Whether you crave convenience or don’t mind a short walk, our app guides you to the perfect spot.

Notification Alerts

Worried about overstaying your reservation? Fear not! Our app sends real-time notifications when your reservation time is exceeded. You won’t be charged for the first 15 minutes after the notification. Beyond that, a small extra fee applies upon exit.
